# A Theoretical and Practical Introduction to Neural Language Models: Evaluating and Exploring their Linguistic Abilities

This repository contains the slides and the materials for the " A Theoretical and Practical Introduction to Neural Language Models: Evaluating and Exploring their Linguistic Abilities" class, organized in the context of the [Autumn School in AI of the PhD Program in Digital Humanities (University of Genova)](https://digitalhumanities.phd.unige.it/node/1230).

* slides: the slides of the lesson.
* notebooks: the notebooks for the practical sessions.
